ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS
================================================================================
Initial Design Pattern:
  - Derivers focused on detecting "problems"
  - Asymmetric coverage: "Worsened" insights existed, "Improved" missing
  - Status vs. Count confusion: Different event types conflated
  - No mutual exclusion: Risk of double-emission on transitions

Systematic Issue:
  All 5 gaps stemmed from treating monitoring as unidirectional (→)
  rather than bidirectional (↔) state machines.

Why This Matters:
  - Automated recovery: Can't trigger if recovery isn't detected
  - Progress visibility: Teams don't see improvement
  - Complete narratives: Can't track broken→fixed→broken chains
  - Event-driven rules: Status transitions enable precise automation
